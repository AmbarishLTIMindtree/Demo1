Digital Experience
The digital experience layer includes two primary user interface(UI) components.
FlexCards-Display contextual information and provide access to relevant tasks for the displayed data.
OmniScripts-A guided path to complete a business process.-multi screen path


the service management layer
The service management layer includes data services that read, write, transform, calculate,and track data within and outside of salesforce.

Dataraptors- configurable services for retrieving, transforming and updating data
Integration Procedures- Declarative, server-side processes that execute multiple actions in a single server call
